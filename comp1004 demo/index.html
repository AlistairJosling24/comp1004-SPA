About Us

VitalPlan is a single page easy access application centered around allowing users to easily plan their daily activities. Our plan creation algorithm is backed by scientififc research and tailored to the users age , gender and overall daily priorities. After each plan is created you will have the opportunity to modify it to better suit your needs. However you will be provided with feedback on each plan made, advising you on what changes to make to your routine to benefit your health. Please take into concideration the feedback you recieve as it is all based on reasearch and is focused your wellbeing. Enjoy!
